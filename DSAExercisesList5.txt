Exercise 3:
A manufacturer's lamps have a 10% chance of burning before 100 hours. 
Four lamps are selected at random and tested for 100 hours. 
What is the probability that at most one of the lamps will burn out? 
Let Y be the number of lamps burned. Y has a binomial distribution with parameters n = 4 and p = 0.1.
Tip: Use the pdf () function.
